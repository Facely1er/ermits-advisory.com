### Assessment Approach

- **Approach**: Quantitative and qualitative analysis
- **Standards Referenced**:

### Risk Rating Scale

| Rating | Likelihood x Impact | Description |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|
| **CRITICAL** | Very High x Catastrophic | Existential threat to organization |
| **HIGH** | High x Severe OR Very High x Moderate | Significant business impact |
| **MEDIUM** | Moderate x Severe OR High x Moderate | Noticeable business impact |
| **LOW** | Low x Severe OR Any x Minor | Minimal business impact |
