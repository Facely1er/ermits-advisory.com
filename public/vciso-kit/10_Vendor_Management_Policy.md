# Vendor Management Policy

**[ORGANIZATION_NAME]**

---

## 1. Purpose

Establish requirements for managing vendors, from procurement through contract management and off-boarding.

---

## 2. Scope

All vendors providing products, services, or access to organizational systems.

---

## 3. Vendor Procurement

### 3.1 Approval Process
1. Business requirement identified
2. Vendor RFQ sent (includes security requirements)
3. Vendor assessment completed
4. Security review performed
5. Executive approval obtained
6. Contract negotiated and signed

### 3.2 Security in RFQ
All RFQs must include:
- Security requirement specification
- Compliance standards (SOC 2, ISO 27001, etc.)
- Incident response expectations
- Data protection requirements
- Audit and assessment rights

### 3.3 Vendor Evaluation Criteria
- **Security** (30%): Controls, certifications, practices
- **Functionality** (40%): Meets business needs
- **Cost** (20%): Competitive pricing
- **Support** (10%): Quality and responsiveness

---

## 4. Vendor Onboarding

### 4.1 Pre-Implementation
- Security agreement signed
- Data processing addendum (if applicable)
- Access credentials provisioned
- Training completed
- System testing performed

### 4.2 Implementation
- Phased approach preferred
- Rollback plan if issues
- Data migration tested
- Performance validated
- Staff trained

### 4.3 Go-Live
- Production access granted
- Monitoring enabled
- Support escalation procedures
- Issue tracking established
- Success metrics defined

---

## 5. Contract Management

### 5.1 Contract Requirements
- Term clearly defined
- Renewal provisions
- Termination clauses
- Cost schedules
- Service level agreements
- Security requirements
- Liability limitations

### 5.2 SLA Monitoring
- Weekly/monthly reporting
- Performance against metrics
- Issue tracking
- Escalation procedures
- Penalty enforcement

### 5.3 Contract Renewal
- Assessment 90 days before expiration
- Performance review
- Cost negotiation
- Updated security requirements
- Renewal decision documentation

---

## 6. Vendor Performance Management

### 6.1 Key Performance Indicators
- System uptime/availability
- Response time to issues
- Security incident response
- SLA compliance rate
- Customer satisfaction scores

### 6.2 Regular Review Meetings
- Monthly: Issue review
- Quarterly: Performance review
- Semi-annual: Strategic review
- Annual: Full assessment

### 6.3 Performance Issues
- Documented and tracked
- Remediation plan required
- Follow-up verification
- Escalation if unresolved
- Termination consideration if critical

---

## 7. Cost Management

### 7.1 Budget Planning
- Annual vendor costs tracked
- Multi-year financial commitments identified
- Cost optimization opportunities
- Budget variance analysis

### 7.2 Invoice Verification
- Invoice review for accuracy
- SLA compliance verification
- Discount verification
- Timely payment processing

---

## 8. Communication and Escalation

### 8.1 Escalation Contacts
- **Tier 1:** Vendor support line
- **Tier 2:** Vendor account manager
- **Tier 3:** Vendor executive sponsor
- **Tier 4:** Legal engagement

### 8.2 Communication Frequency
- Weekly: Operational issues
- Monthly: Status review
- Quarterly: Business review
- Annual: Executive alignment

---

## 9. Vendor Off-boarding

### 9.1 Transition Planning
- Advance notice (per contract, minimum 30 days)
- Transition plan required
- Knowledge transfer schedule
- Data return/destruction plan
- Cutover procedure

### 9.2 Off-boarding Checklist
- [ ] Transition plan submitted
- [ ] Staff trained on new system
- [ ] Data migration completed
- [ ] System access revoked
- [ ] Equipment returned
- [ ] Final invoices paid
- [ ] Contract formally closed
- [ ] Lessons learned documented

### 9.3 Data Handling
- Data returned in agreed format
- Data securely destroyed (with certificate)
- Compliance verified
- Documentation retained

---

## 10. Compliance and Audits

### 10.1 Audit Rights
- Right to audit security controls
- On-site assessments permitted
- Document reviews
- Interview access
- Frequency per criticality

### 10.2 Audit Planning
- Annual audit schedule
- Audit scope clearly defined
- Vendor notification in advance
- Audit findings documented
- Remediation tracking

### 10.3 Compliance Verification
- Certifications reviewed annually
- Compliance attestations obtained
- Incident disclosures tracked
- Regulatory changes monitored
