## NIST CSF Overview

The NIST Cybersecurity Framework consists of:
- **5 Core Functions**: Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover
- **23 Categories**: Groupings of cybersecurity activities
- **Outcomes**: Desired results for each category
- **Implementation Tiers**: Maturity levels (Partial → Optimized)

### Maturity Scale
- **1 (Partial)**: Process is ad hoc, inconsistent, reactive
- **2 (Progressing)**: Process is partially defined, somewhat consistent
- **3 (Repeatable)**: Process is documented, consistently implemented
- **4 (Optimized)**: Process is fully automated, optimized, risk-based
