# STEEL™ Assessment Platform

**Strategic Threat Environment Evaluation Landscape**

## Overview
Executive-level cybersecurity assessment integrating PESTEL analysis with threat landscape intelligence, aligned with ISO 31000, COSO ERM, and NIST frameworks.

## Features
- **46 comprehensive questions** across 6 PESTEL factors
- **Enhanced ERM alignment** with risk appetite and business continuity questions
- **Modern security architecture** assessment (Zero Trust, Supply Chain)
- **Automated scoring** with weighted calculations
- **Results dashboard** with risk level indicators and interpretation guide
- **Strategic recommendations** with next steps and risk register integration
- **PDF and JSON export** functionality
- **Privacy-first** client-side processing with SRI-protected CDN resources

## Framework Alignment
- ISO 31000:2018 - Risk Management Guidelines
- COSO ERM Framework - Enterprise Risk Management
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0
- NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF)
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 - Information Security Management

## Question Distribution
- Political: 8 questions (including Risk Appetite)
- Economic: 7 questions
- Social: 7 questions
- Technological: 9 questions (including Zero Trust & Supply Chain)
- Environmental: 8 questions (including BC/DR)
- Legal: 7 questions
