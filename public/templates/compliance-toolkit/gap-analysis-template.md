## Gap Analysis Matrix

### Instructions
For each control area, rate your current implementation:
- **5 = Fully Implemented** - Control is fully operational with evidence
- **4 = Mostly Implemented** - Control is operational but needs minor improvements
- **3 = Partially Implemented** - Control exists but significant gaps remain
- **2 = Minimally Implemented** - Control is in early stages
- **1 = Not Implemented** - Control does not exist
