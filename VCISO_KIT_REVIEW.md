## ⚠️ Redundancy Analysis

### 1. Incident Response Playbooks - OVERLAP

**vCISO Kit Contains:**
- 5 incident response playbooks (Ransomware, Data Breach, Credential Compromise, Malware Outbreak, Insider Threat)

**Incident Response Toolkit Contains:**
- 6+ scenario-specific playbooks (Ransomware, Data Breach, DDoS, Credential Compromise, Insider Threat, Phishing)
- Communication templates (12+)
- Incident tracking forms
- Tabletop exercise templates

**Redundancy Level:** 🔴 **HIGH**
- 4 out of 5 playbooks overlap (Ransomware, Data Breach, Credential Compromise, Insider Threat)
- vCISO Kit playbooks are more basic/policy-focused
- Incident Response Toolkit playbooks are more detailed/operational

**Recommendation:**
- **Option A:** Keep both but differentiate:
  - vCISO Kit = High-level policy playbooks (strategic)
  - Incident Response Toolkit = Detailed operational playbooks (tactical)
- **Option B:** Remove playbooks from vCISO Kit, focus on policies/board materials
- **Option C:** Make vCISO Kit playbooks reference Incident Response Toolkit for detailed procedures
