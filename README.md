## 🔬 What is STEEL™?

**STEEL™** (Strategic Threat & Enterprise Evaluation Layer) is ERMITS Corporation's proprietary intelligence engine that transforms fragmented risk signals into unified, actionable enterprise resilience intelligence.

### How STEEL Works

STEEL evaluates organizations across **six PESTEL factors**:

| Factor | Focus Area | Weighting |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| **Political** | Regulatory compliance, policy changes, government relations | 1.0x |
| **Economic** | Budget impact, financial risks, market conditions | 1.0x |
| **Social** | Human factors, organizational culture, stakeholder awareness | 1.0x |
| **Technological** | Innovation threats, digital transformation, emerging risks | **1.2x** |
| **Environmental** | Sustainability, ESG compliance, climate-related cyber risks | 0.9x |
| **Legal** | Legal requirements, data protection, liability management | **1.1x** |

### STEEL Assessment Process

1. **42-Question Executive Assessment** (25-30 minutes)
   - 7 questions per PESTEL factor
   - Scored 1-10 per question
   - Industry-calibrated scoring

2. **Composite Risk Scoring** (0-100 scale)
   - Weighted composite score across all factors
   - Individual factor breakdowns
   - Risk categorization (Low/Moderate/Elevated/High)

3. **Strategic Recommendations**
   - Prioritized by factor gaps
   - Quick-win identification (30/60/90 day)
   - ERMITS platform recommendations
   - Industry benchmarking

4. **Executive Deliverables**
   - Board-ready presentation
   - PDF executive report
   - Visual dashboards and charts
   - Implementation roadmap

### Why STEEL is Different

**Traditional Approach:**
- Fragmented tools (SecurityScorecard, OneTrust, Prevalent)
- Incompatible scoring systems
- No unified risk view
- Manual correlation required

**STEEL Approach:**
- Single unified intelligence engine
- Consistent scoring methodology
- Holistic business risk view
- Automated cross-domain correlation
- Native framework alignment (NIST, ISO, GDPR, CMMC, ESG)
