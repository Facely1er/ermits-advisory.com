# ERMITS Advisory Revision Summary
## What Changed and Why

**Date:** November 14, 2025  
**Revision Purpose:** Resource optimization and scope refinement

---

## 🎯 STRATEGIC CHANGES

### REMOVED SERVICES (For Resource Optimization)

#### 1. M&A Cyber Due Diligence Services
**Original Offering:**
- M&A Cyber Due Diligence ($25K-$100K)
- Deal assessment and integration planning
- Post-merger security roadmap
- Risk quantification for transactions

**Why Removed:**
- Requires specialized M&A expertise
- Resource-intensive (80-120 hours per engagement)
- Limited scalability
- Outside core STEEL-to-Platform focus
- Would dilute advisory team from core competencies

**Alternative Approach:**
- Partner with M&A-focused security firms
- Refer clients for specialized due diligence
- Focus on post-acquisition security program building (within scope)

#### 2. Training & Certification Programs
**Original Offering:**
- ERMITS Academy programs
- Executive education workshops
- Professional certifications
- Board governance training
- CISO leadership development
- Security awareness campaigns

**Why Removed:**
- Training delivery is operationally different from advisory
- Requires dedicated curriculum development
- Certification program maintenance is resource-intensive
- Not directly connected to STEEL or platform adoption
- Better handled by specialized training partners

**Alternative Approach:**
- Partner with cybersecurity training providers
- Include basic user training as part of platform implementation
- Focus advisory on strategic guidance, not training delivery
- Refer clients to partners for comprehensive training programs

## 💡 STRATEGIC RATIONALE SUMMARY

**Why This Revision Makes Sense:**

1. **Focus = Excellence:** By removing M&A and training, advisory team can become world-class at STEEL-powered strategic advisory

2. **Platform Integration = Differentiation:** Deep integration with ERMITS ecosystem creates unmatched value proposition

3. **Resource Efficiency = Scalability:** Smaller, focused team can serve more clients with better outcomes

4. **Recurring Revenue = Predictability:** vCISO retainers and continuous advisory create stable cash flow

5. **Clear Pathway = Higher Conversion:** STEEL → Platform → Advisory is simple to understand and sell

6. **Measurable Outcomes = Proof:** STEEL scoring and platform metrics provide tangible value demonstration

7. **Mid-Market Focus = Volume:** Large addressable market with urgent needs and appropriate budgets

**Result:** Sustainable, scalable, high-margin advisory practice that drives ERMITS ecosystem adoption
