## 🎯 SERVICE OVERVIEW

### Three-Tier vCISO Model

#### **Strategic Advisory vCISO**
- **Monthly Investment:** $8,000 - $15,000
- **Time Commitment:** 10-15 hours per month
- **Ideal For:** Organizations needing executive guidance and board support
- **Delivery Model:** Virtual/remote with quarterly on-site (optional)

#### **Operational vCISO**
- **Monthly Investment:** $18,000 - $35,000
- **Time Commitment:** 25-45 hours per month
- **Ideal For:** Organizations needing program management and team leadership
- **Delivery Model:** Hybrid (2-3 days/week remote, 1 day on-site monthly)

#### **Executive vCISO**
- **Monthly Investment:** $40,000 - $70,000
- **Time Commitment:** 80+ hours per month
- **Ideal For:** Organizations needing near full-time CISO leadership
- **Delivery Model:** On-site 2-3 days/week + remote availability

## 🚀 CLIENT ONBOARDING PROCESS

### Phase 1: Pre-Contract (Week -2 to -1)

#### **Initial Discovery Call (60 minutes)**

**Agenda Template:**
```
1. Introduction & Relationship Building (10 min)
   - vCISO background and expertise
   - Client stakeholder introductions
   - Establish rapport

2. Current State Assessment (20 min)
   - Organization overview (size, industry, locations)
   - Current security posture and challenges
   - Existing team and resources
   - Compliance requirements
   - Recent incidents or concerns

3. Needs Analysis (15 min)
   - Immediate pain points
   - Strategic objectives
   - Board/executive expectations
   - Budget and timeline constraints

4. Service Fit & Tier Recommendation (10 min)
   - Recommended vCISO tier
   - ERMITS platform recommendations
   - Expected outcomes
   - Investment discussion

5. Next Steps & Timeline (5 min)
   - Proposal timeline
   - Questions and concerns
   - Meeting schedule planning
```

**Discovery Call Checklist:**
- [ ] Company overview document received
- [ ] Org chart reviewed
- [ ] Current security documentation reviewed (if available)
- [ ] Compliance requirements documented
- [ ] Key stakeholders identified
- [ ] Success criteria defined
- [ ] Budget range confirmed

#### **Proposal Development (2-3 days)**

**Proposal Template Sections:**

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Client challenges identified
   - Recommended solution (tier + platform)
   - Expected outcomes
   - Investment summary

2. **Scope of Services**
   - Detailed deliverables by tier
   - Communication rhythms
   - Platform integration plan
   - Timeline and milestones

3. **vCISO Approach & Methodology**
   - STEEL framework integration
   - 90-day roadmap preview
   - Governance model
   - Escalation procedures

4. **Team & Qualifications**
   - vCISO advisor profile
   - Relevant industry experience
   - Certifications and credentials
   - Client references

5. **ERMITS Platform Benefits**
   - Included platform tools
   - Integration approach
   - Efficiency gains
   - Reporting capabilities

6. **Investment & Terms**
   - Monthly retainer fee
   - Contract duration (typically 12 months)
   - Payment terms
   - Termination clauses

7. **Success Metrics**
   - STEEL score improvement targets
   - Compliance milestones
   - Incident reduction goals
   - Team maturity advancement

**Proposal Review Meeting (30 minutes)**
- Walk through proposal
- Address questions and concerns
- Negotiate terms if needed
- Confirm next steps

#### **Contract Execution**

**Required Documents:**

**Agenda Template:**
```

   - All stakeholder introductions

   - Communication preferences

2. Engagement Overview (20 min)
   - Service tier review
   - Deliverables and expectations
   - Communication rhythms
   - Escalation procedures
   - Success metrics

   - Organization structure and culture
   - Current security initiatives
   - Technology stack overview
   - Existing policies and procedures
   - Team capabilities and gaps
   - Budget and resources

4. Initial Assessment Plan (20 min)
   - 30-day assessment activities
   - Required meetings and interviews
   - Documentation review needs
   - System access requirements
   - STEEL assessment timeline

5. ERMITS Platform Onboarding (15 min)
   - Platform overview and demo
   - User access provisioning
   - Initial configuration discussion
   - Training schedule

6. 30/60/90 Day Roadmap (15 min)
```

- [ ] Platform access verified
- [ ] First weekly/monthly meeting scheduled
