## 💰 PRICING GUIDANCE

### Tier Selection Criteria

**Strategic Advisory vCISO ($8K-$15K/month):**
**Best For:**
- Companies with existing security team needing strategic guidance
- Organizations preparing for board-level governance
- Businesses needing compliance strategy and oversight
- Teams that need executive air cover and support

**Red Flags:**
- No security team (need Operational tier)
- Daily operational crises (need Operational/Executive)
- Complex compliance requirements (need Operational)
- Expecting hands-on work (need Operational/Executive)

---

**Operational vCISO ($18K-$35K/month):**
**Best For:**
- Mid-market companies building security programs
- Organizations with small security teams
- Companies pursuing compliance certifications
- Businesses needing program management

**Red Flags:**
- Only need strategic advice (downgrade to Strategic)
- Need full-time on-site presence (upgrade to Executive)
- Budget constraints vs. expectations mismatch
- Expecting 24/7 availability (upgrade to Executive)

---

**Executive vCISO ($40K-$70K/month):**
**Best For:**
- Organizations in transformation or high growth
- Companies with mature security needs
- Businesses requiring on-site presence
- Enterprises with complex programs

**Red Flags:**
- Limited budget (consider Operational tier)
- Simple security needs (downgrade to appropriate tier)
- Not utilizing near full-time commitment
- Better served by full-time hire (>$70K/month)
